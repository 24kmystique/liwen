CUP Q

CUP Q

Adobe Illustrator
Design Framework
User Journery Map

Description

CUP Q is a multi-functional cup that encourages user to Bring Your Own Bottle (BYOB) to purchase a takeaway drink instead of consuming one-time use plastic cups. This was a 14-week group project where SUTD students were tasked to solved an identified problem in the school community.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Remove the top bottle.
  • Step 2: Purchase drink using the bottom cup.
  • Step 3: Drink stall owners will scan the QR code.
  • Step 4: Pay using the QR code.

Inspiration

There have been many environmental solutions that promotes people to BYOB such as restricting drink stall owners from providing straws or collapsible cup where its portability and compact design is used to motivate user to bring their cup anywhere they go. These solutions range from gadgets to policies however, BYOB is still not a common practice. We hope this inspires SUTD community to adopt more enviromentally-friendly habits.
